Welcome to LIPID+

LIPID+ (Lipid Identification and Profiling for Intelligent Discovery) is a machine learning powered platform designed specifically for mass spectrometry based lipidomics. It overcomes the failures of traditional metabolomics tools in lipidomics, by shifting focus from ambiguous full structures to reliably identifiable Principal Lipid Structural Features (PLSFs).

Core Features

  • MS Search: A large-scale, curated lipid database containing lipids from diverse sources (represented as PLSF), along with their precursor m/z values and corresponding MS² spectra, enables high-throughput mass spectral searching.
  • De Novo Generation: For spectra that cannot be identified through database matching, the SLAM (Spectrum-based Lipid Annotation Model) module supports de novo generation of lipid PLSFs.
  • System-level Analysis: Tailored analytical and visualization tools designed specifically for lipid characteristics allow detailed exploration of lipid-specific structural features—such as chain length and degree of unsaturation—capturing their dynamic changes. The analytical results are presented as interactive and easily shareable HTML reports.
